Design a Natural pH Indicator

In this lab experience, students prepare equimolar solutions of weak acids and measure their pH values to rank the acids in order of increasing strength. Students come to understand that the weak acid with the lowest pH is relatively the strongest acid, and the position of its equilibrium lies farthest to the right. Editable, differentiated instructions range from a time-sensitive prescriptive lab to full open inquiry, and robust online videos and content—including a virtual reality (VR) simulation—help students prepare for and better understand the labs they’re conducting.
